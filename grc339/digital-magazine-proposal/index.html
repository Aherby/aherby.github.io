Introduction

Mellow Bars

A magazine focused on providing a platform for lesser know musical artists. Specifically R&B and hip-hop artists who have a sound that's jazz infused. The site will have both opinion articles with listening reccommendations as well as news about upcoming releases, historical information relating to the genre, and tools to help people make amazing new playlists.
